Types of Outfit

Casual Wear

This includes jeans, sweaters, t - shirts, sneakers, and much more. For women, this means casual, yet semi-conservative dresses like wrap dresses, loose-fitting summer dresses, linen pants and casual short-sleeve collar-shirts on hot summer days.

Formal Wear

At its most basic, contemporary formal dress generally encompasses both white tie and black tie dress codes. Semi-formal, as the name implies, is slightly more relaxed – think a dark suit and a tie, or anything you'd refer to as “cocktail” dress.

Ethnic Wear

Ethnic fashion is inspired by the culture where it originates. It is fashion that embraces and represents a culture and allows one to celebrate her heritage and origin. Ethnic means that something shares the distinctive cultural traits of a group in society.
